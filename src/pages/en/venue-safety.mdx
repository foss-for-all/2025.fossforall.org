# Transportation Guide

## Subway

### Line 1
Incheon, Suwon → Seoul Station → Cheongnyangni → Uijeongbu direction > Get off at Kwangwoon University Station, 6-minute walk

### Line 6
Eungam → Bonghwasan direction > Get off at Seokgye Station, 10-minute walk from Exit 1

### Line 7
When using Dobong → Nowon → Gunja → Konkuk University → Cheongdam → Terminal → Onsu, transfer to Line 6 at Taereung Station, get off at Seokgye Station, 10-minute walk from Exit 1
